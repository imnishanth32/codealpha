What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyberattack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information like passwords or credit card details.

How to Recognize Phishing

  • ⚠️ Suspicious email addresses
  • ⚠️ Urgent messages ("Act now!")
  • ⚠️ Fake links
  • ⚠️ Grammar mistakes

Social Engineering Tactics

Attackers manipulate emotions:

  • Fear ("Account blocked!")
  • Urgency ("Immediate action required")
  • Authority ("CEO request")
  • Curiosity ("You've won!")

Best Practices

  • ✅ Verify sender emails
  • ✅ Use 2FA
  • ✅ Avoid clicking unknown links
  • ✅ Keep software updated
